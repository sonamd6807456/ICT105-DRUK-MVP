# Customer Discovery Questions

### Smart Campus Event Hub

* 1. How do you currently find out about campus events?

* 2. Have you ever missed a campus event? Why?

* 3. What platforms do you use to receive event information?

* 4. What challenges do you face when searching for campus events?

* 5. How often do you participate in campus activities?

* 6. What information is most important before attending an event?

* 7. Do you think event information is easy to access? Why or why not?

* 8. What improvements would help you discover events more easily?

* 9. How do event organizers usually promote events?

* 10. Would you use a centralized campus event platform? Why?
