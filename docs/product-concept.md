# Product Concept

## 1. Project Title
Smart Campus Event Hub

## 2. Target User
**Primary Users**:
First-year undergraduate students at Rangsit University who frequently miss campus events because information is scattered across multiple communication channels.
International and exchange students who are unfamiliar with university communication channels and need a centralized source of campus information.

**Secondary Users**:
Student clubs and organizations that create and promote campus events.
Campus administrators responsible for publishing university announcements and managing campus activities.

## 3. Problem Statement
University students often miss campus events, workshops, competitions, club activities, and important deadlines because event information is distributed across multiple platforms such as social media, messaging applications, emails, posters, and university websites. This fragmented communication makes it difficult for students to stay informed, resulting in missed learning opportunities, reduced participation, and lower engagement in campus life.

## 4. Proposed IT Product
The Smart Campus Event Hub is a web-based platform that centralizes campus event information into a single, user-friendly system. Students can browse upcoming events, search and filter activities, view complete event details, register online, and receive timely reminders and notifications. Student clubs and campus administrators can publish, update, and manage event information through an organized dashboard.

## 5. Core Value Proposition
The Smart Campus Event Hub simplifies how students discover and participate in university activities by providing one reliable platform for campus events. It reduces missed opportunities, improves communication between organizers and students, increases participation in university programs, and creates a more connected campus community.

## 6. MVP Scope
The Minimum Viable Product (MVP) will include the following core features:

*User-friendly homepage displaying upcoming campus events.
*Centralized event listing with search and filtering by category, date, and organizer.
*Detailed event information page.
*Online event registration.
*Event reminders and notifications.
*Calendar view of upcoming activities.
*Organizer dashboard for publishing and managing events.
*Basic participant registration management.

## 7. Out of Scope for Current Semester
The following features are not included in the MVP and may be considered for future development:

*AI-powered personalized event recommendations.
*Integration with university student information systems.
*QR code attendance tracking.
*Mobile application (Android/iOS).
*Real-time chat and messaging between participants and organizers.
*Advanced analytics and reporting dashboard.
*Multi-university event integration.
*Multilingual interface beyond the initial implementation.
